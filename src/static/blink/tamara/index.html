Brand New Guys has been tasked to produce a campaign to give voice to Oxfam Novib's brand story. Rather than building this from an ivory tower, we've organized a gathering of voices that matter, to explore what our shared future should look like.
We’re inviting community leaders to share the values they believe should guide us moving forward, and we want you to be part of this conversation.
We’ll kick off the evening with drinks and bites to set the tone, then dive into an open, meaningful discussion, led and designed by Yootha Wong-Loi-Sing (YFA)
and Caroline Halvardsson (Spotify).
The process will be documented to capture the ideas, but you can opt out of being included if you’d prefer. This is about building a future together— we’d love to have you with us.
